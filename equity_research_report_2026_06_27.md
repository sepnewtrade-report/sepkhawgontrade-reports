Each stock is evaluated across five core pillars, scored from 0 to 20, totaling up to 100 points:

1. **Fundamental Score (0-20):** Evaluates business quality, market share, balance sheet health, and pricing power.

3. **Technical Score (0-20):** Evaluates moving average alignments, support/resistance, breakouts, and pattern quality.

5. **Momentum Score (0-20):** Evaluates relative strength index (RSI), rate of change (ROC), and volume expansion.
